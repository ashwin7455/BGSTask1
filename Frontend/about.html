Overview

Established in 2014, Bridge Group Solutions (BGS) has grown from a niche project outsourcing company into a trusted name in digital transformation, enterprise solutions, and strategic consulting across India. Headquartered in Delhi NCR with a presence in Mumbai, Uttar Pradesh, and Tier-2 cities, BGS serves as a reliable growth partner for startups, SMEs, and large enterprises.

Our strength lies in building expert-driven teams, implementing scalable technologies, and maintaining standardized yet flexible delivery models. Every solution is designed to solve real business challenges, streamline operations, and unlock long-term growth.

    With over a decade of operational excellence, BGS specializes in:


  • • Custom ERP & Software Development
  • • Wealth & Portfolio Management
  • • Risk Advisory Solutions
  • • Strategic Talent Outsourcing & Project Execution

In less than two years, B.G.S has expanded to over five cities and continues to grow at an impressive pace. Here, aspiration meets innovation, candor fuels collaboration, and teamwork makes the impossible possible.
We are proud of our exceptional client retention rate, built through referrals and enduring partnerships. Guided by our "True North" — an unwavering commitment to doing the right thing — we work hand-in-hand with clients, turning bold ambitions into extraordinary results.
