A Brief History of ERP

Since its origins in the 1960s as inventory management software for manufacturers, ERP has evolved into an enterprise-critical platform.

From Material Requirements Planning (MRP) systems in the 1970s to integrated MRP-II in the 1980s, and finally to the comprehensive ERP suites coined by Gartner in 1990, these systems now power operational agility, enterprise visibility, and strategic decision-making worldwide.”
