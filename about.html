Overview

Founded in 2014, B.G.S is one of India's original project outsourcing start-ups, created to bridge gaps in process management and outsourcing. Starting as a project outsourcing firm, we quickly expanded into technology, wealth management, and talent management solutions — always with a focus on delivering high-quality, cost-effective, and practical interventions. Our strength lies in building experienced teams, developing technological enablers, and institutionalizing standardized processes. Strong research, consistent outperformance, and high standards of client service are testaments to our robust model.

In less than two years, B.G.S has expanded to over five cities and continues to grow at an impressive pace. Here, aspiration meets innovation, candor fuels collaboration, and teamwork makes the impossible possible. We are proud of our exceptional client retention rate, built through referrals and enduring partnerships. Guided by our "True North" — an unwavering commitment to doing the right thing — we work hand-in-hand with clients, turning bold ambitions into extraordinary results. At B.G.S, we don’t just meet standards — we set new ones.
